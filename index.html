CLIENT SERVICES

Reconnaissance Survey

Defined as a survey with little to no subsurface testing. We can perform an initial reconnaissance survey of large project areas in order to assess field conditions and obvious disturbances within the study area. Information acquired during a reconnaissance survey is be used to formulate a realistic project schedule and budget.

Phase I Intensive Cultural Resources Inventories

Intensive Phase I survey is the only reliable method for achieving maximum coverage of a study area in which cultural resources (archeological sites) may suffer adverse impact. It is designed to locate previously unreported cultural resources and to relocate previously reported cultural resources.
